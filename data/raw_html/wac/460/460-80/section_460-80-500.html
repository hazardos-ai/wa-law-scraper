Agency filings affecting this section

PDFWAC 460-80-500

Advertising—Filing requirement—Timing.

All advertising to be used to offer a franchise, subject to the registration requirement, for sale must be filed in the office of the director at least seven days prior to the publication.
[Statutory Authority: Chapter 19.100 RCW, RCW 19.100.250, 19.100.010, 19.100.030, 19.100.040, 19.100.050, 19.100.070, 19.100.080, 19.100.100, and 19.100.110. WSR 09-22-050, § 460-80-500, filed 10/29/09, effective 11/29/09; Order 11, § 460-80-500, filed 3/3/72.]
